Just occasionally you come across a contractor who really does go above and beyond expectations…and that is exactly what I experienced with Ashley and Renegade Electricals. After several years of obtaining quotes for PV installations for my 1930’s bungalow, I gained a poor impression of solar providers. Main issue was I had a difficult roof with various obstructions which made the positioning of PV panels problematic. Most companies that estimated did so from a Google maps satellite view which resulted in quotes for six/eight panels when I required 10 for an economically viable system. Many of these companies had a poor reputation, were expensive and I didn’t find their business model reassuring. Eventually I contacted Octopus who were offering PV solutions. However, they only want to do simple jobs – getting a modular kit out the back of a van and sticking it on an obstacle free south-facing roof, all done in a day. So, clearly this wouldn’t suit my irregular roof. But Octopus did recommend certain installers in the area who may be able to quote if you didn’t fit their simple “flat-pack” model. And this is where Renegade Electricals appeared. Ashley was brilliant. He came to the house, measured up the roof, noting the obstacles and establishing workarounds. Consulting with his roofer colleagues, he believed that it would be possible to install 10 panels utilising both south/west facing roofs, explaining that (understandably) it would be a bit of a dog’s dinner. But we were okay with this, it was the only way after all. Ashley also reassured us with his business model, explaining that all payments were insurance backed by the HIES scheme which covers you in the event of a business going bankrupt. Having been a previous victim of this sort of Builders scam I found this very encouraging and consequently elected to use Ashley as my chosen contractor. I felt I could trust him. I soon received written quotations, an explanation of the HIES scheme and projections of what the PV installation was going to generate in the coming years. I got the impression I was dealing with a competent professional who knew his business. A week or two later the scaffolders arrived and completed their work in a couple of hours and the following day Ashley turned up with his cohort Clayton and they began the electrical work in the garage, positioning and installing the battery/inverter. Having quoted for 2 x 4kW batteries, Ashley had phoned me from the wholesalers to inform me that there was a new 9.6kW battery available, with built-in heater (handy in my cold garage, reduces battery discharge) for not much more money so I elected to go for that. A great piece of customer service there. The following day the roofers turned up and installed the 10 panels exactly as Ashley had planned. Okay, they don’t look pretty not having the asymmetry of a typical rectangular arrangement, but it is far better than we could have hoped for. On the final day, commissioning, they got the system running only to run into a couple of technical issues which took some time to resolve. But Ashley worked with Alpha-ESS technical to establish the fault and resolve it. Once up and running I have to say I’m delighted with the installation. It looks neat and professional and the whole experience, taking around five/six weeks in total, was very straightforward. The kit Ashley provided from Alpha-ESS appears top quality and the app for monitoring the kit is very intuitive. I was provided with the requisite certification required to register with Octopus and that process was quickly sorted. I’m now very glad that I took the decision to go ahead with the installation having prevaricated for some time due to my mistrust of the profession and contractors in general – Ashley has turned this around for me. In conclusion Ashley and Clayton were consummate professionals, very reasonably priced and I can wholeheartedly recommend them. I wish them well in their future business and I’ll be using them again in the Spring for a full electrical test/inspection.
